# CMS User Guide

## 1. Accessing the Admin Panel
1. Go to `https://yoursite.com/admin`.
2. Click **Login with Netlify Identity**.
3. Use the credentials provided by your developer or sign up if registration is open.

## 2. Editing Content
### Homepage
- Go to **Collections > Pages > Home Page**.
- Edit the Title, Badge, Description, and CTA Button texts.
- **Publish**: Click "Publish" at the top to save changes live.

### About Page
- Go to **Collections > Pages > About Page**.
- Edit Director's Quote, Image, and Company Description.
- Update the "Stats" section by adding or removing items.

### Managing Services
- Go to **Collections > Services**.
- Create New: Click "New Service".
- Edit Existing: Click on a service to edit title, description, or icon.

### Managing Projects
- Go to **Collections > Projects**.
- Upload project images and fill in details (Location, Capacity, Category).

## 3. Contact Form Submissions
- All inquiries from the website form are sent directly to the **Netlify Forms** dashboard.
- You can configure email notifications in Netlify under **Site Settings > Forms > Form Notifications**.

## 4. Updates & Publishing
- When you click **Publish**, the changes are saved to the Git repository.
- Netlify automatically detects the change and rebuilds the website.
- It usually takes 1-2 minutes for changes to appear live.
